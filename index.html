The Problem

Marketing spends money. Sales can't prove ROI.

Phone-based businesses rely on reps asking "How did you hear about us?" The result? Broken attribution data that makes it impossible to measure marketing ROI.

In most phone-heavy businesses, lead-source tracking is a mess:

  • Reps forget to ask or guess the answer just to move on
  • Manual entries are inconsistent across the team
  • Fields are left blank or filled with generic values like "website"
  • No way to connect actual caller words to marketing campaigns

Without accurate attribution, marketing budgets get wasted on channels that don't work, and teams can't scale what actually drives revenue.

The consequences:

  • Marketing can't prove which campaigns drive closed deals
  • Sales leaders can't trust their pipeline reports
  • Budgets flow to the wrong channels based on gut feeling
  • Growing teams can't identify what's working to replicate it
The Solution

Turn every call into reliable revenue intelligence

Closer AI analyzes sales call recordings with AI to extract true lead sources, referral signals, and key outcomes—then automatically syncs structured data to your CRM.

How it works:

  • Advanced speech-to-text transcribes every call with high accuracy
  • Semantic AI detects direct and indirect mentions of lead source
  • Extracts structured fields like lead origin, referral source, and call intent
  • Automatically writes clean data back into your CRM in real-time

You get accurate, first-party attribution data without changing your team's workflow or adding manual steps.

What you gain:

  • Accurate marketing attribution for every channel and campaign
  • Eliminate manual data entry errors and missing fields
  • Trust your reports for budget planning and forecasting
  • Foundation for AI sales automation and lead scoring
How It Works

From call recordings to trusted data

Closer AI integrates with your existing phone system and CRM. No rebuild required.

Step 1
Connect your phone system

Integrate with your phone provider (Twilio, RingCentral, etc.) or upload call recordings. We handle secure storage and processing.

Step 2
AI analyzes every conversation

Our models transcribe calls and use semantic AI to identify lead source, referral clues, objections, and outcomes from natural conversation.

Step 3
Auto-sync to your CRM

Closer AI writes structured fields directly into your CRM, so your dashboards and reports always reflect what callers actually said.
